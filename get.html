ROADMAP FOR EVERYONE

Beginner: 1-4 Months

We all start somewhere, and there isn’t a better time to begin than now. So whether you are a child fascinated with music, a teen trying to find their passion, an adult in search of ways to destress or a retiree picking up a life skill; we are here to cater to your needs..

Inter-Mediate:4-8 Months

So you have picked up the basics, and you want to continue your journey into music. We are here for you. Consider this a level up in your skill set. But it’s still a while to go before you master your skill and fascinate the world with it.

ADVANCE:8-12 Months

Almost there, but still a bit away. At the Advanced level you are almost a semi-pro. You now know your path, and that is why you understand why this important step is the difference between you stepping out into the big leagues or not.

Professional:12-16 Months

You are here, you have arrived. And you have done this with sheer hard work, practice and determination. Now it’s time to play on a higher level, gain mastery of your craft, and walk out into the world with the confidence that only a skilled professional has.
